Anarock Channel Partner Application

A Unified Platform for Property Listings, CRM, and Commission Management.

Project Overview

Anarock is a B2B Platform for channel partners(brokers) to view listing of properties and sell them to clients. App allows CPs to manage all their clients with the help of CRM which is inbuild in the app and also after selling the property CPs can manage all the finance proceeding in the app itself with the help of CM(commission management). CPs get exciting offers and early commission on the selling of properties with Seal of Trust.

Project type: End-to-end app + branding

Industry

Real Estate Technology (PropTech)

Tools

Figma, FigJam, Clickup & Google Meet

Duration

6 Months

Roles and Responsibilities

Led the entire design process across modules and user levels—translating complex workflows into intuitive, role-sensitive experiences.

Problem Statement

Context & Background

The real estate sector relies heavily on Channel Partners (CPs), yet their workflows remain outdated and fragmented. Anarock recognized the need for a unified digital solution to enhance efficiency, trust, and scalability for both individual brokers and organizations.

  • CPs traditionally use scattered tools and offline methods to manage clients, listings, and commissions.
  • These manual processes result in inefficiencies, lost opportunities, and a lack of professional credibility.
  • There's no single platform tailored to the complete journey of a CP—from onboarding to post-sale finance tracking.
  • Clients often hesitate to trust brokers due to unverified credentials and inconsistent communication.
  • Anarock aimed to bridge this gap by introducing a mobile-first platform to streamline and professionalize CP operations.

User Needs & Business Goals

To ensure the platform serves both Channel Partners and the business effectively, it was essential to align user needs with strategic business goals. The design focuses on solving day-to-day broker challenges while supporting Anarock's vision for scalable growth and trust-driven sales.

User Needs:

  • A simple onboarding flow for both individual CPs and organizations, including verification (e.g., RERA details).
  • A trusted identity tool (Digital Visiting Card) to build credibility with clients.
  • An easy-to-use system to browse, filter, and share property listings with clients.
  • A centralized space to manage leads, track client progress, and monitor commissions.

Business Goals:

  • Digitize the broker ecosystem to increase sales efficiency and reduce lead leakage.
  • Promote verified CPs to build trust and strengthen Anarock's brand reputation.
  • Provide CPs with incentives like early commissions to encourage engagement and loyalty.
  • Enable scalability by supporting both individual and organizational users within one platform..

Final Flows & Designs

Overview of the Final Design

The Anarock Channel Partner Application delivers a streamlined, professional, and scalable platform for real estate brokers to manage listings, clients, and commissions. The design focuses on empowering CPs with tools to boost efficiency, build trust, and grow their business—all through a user-friendly mobile interface.

Key Highlights:

  • Mobile-first B2B platform tailored for real estate Channel Partners (CPs).
  • Smooth onboarding process for both individual CPs and organizations, including RERA verification.
  • Digital Visiting Card feature to present verified identity and build trust with clients.
  • Robust project listing section with filtering, sorting, project details, and sharing capabilities.
  • In-app CRM to track client engagement, sales funnel, and customer lifecycle.
  • Commission Management (CM) to monitor earnings and access early commission offers.
  • Dynamic homepage with nudges, marketing banners, lead summaries, and finance snapshot.
  • Editable user profile section with photo updates and email verification.
  • "My Team" module (for organizations) to add, edit, and manage team members and roles.
  • Consistent, professional UI design with a focus on clarity, usability, and business growth.

Channel Partner(CP)/Organisation Onboarding

First time user will onboard by using number and OTP following with the filling personal details and then continues by choosing between he owns an organisation or he is individual CP. CP also has to fll his organisation details like RERA details.

Digital Visiting Card

Digital Visiting Card is a CP's visiting card which he can share with the potential clients to let them know that he is with the organisation or he has official RERA ID. Here user can create the visiting card by filling all the necessary details and then choose of the templete to select his visiting card. User can also upload the existing visiting card by uploading it.

Project - Filters, Listing, Project Details

CPs can view the Project Listing to view projects toview project details and offers that comes along with projects which they can avail after selling the project. Users can also filter the projects according to their need and also sort the listing to view accordingly. In project details they can share the project details with the client for them to understand in & out of the project.

When the user opens the app he sees Homepage where he can see the nudges which gives him immediate actionables, advetisement section which will help marketing team to market new projects,app features, etc. , Then he gets summary of his customers at differnent stages in CRM,and then finally he sees all the finance summary. User can also view the favorite projects from the top bar of the app. USer also get the more options from side naviagtion.

Profile

Profile contains all the details about the CP which he can edit at any given time. CP can change Profile Pictue from here and verify his email.

My Team

My Team in bottom navigation will only be visible to organisation profile. It will contain all the team members. Team owner and admin can edit the details of team members.

Conclusion

Working on the Anarock Channel Partner Application was a transformative experience in designing for the real estate technology sector. This project allowed me to create a comprehensive B2B platform that bridges the gap between traditional broker workflows and modern digital solutions. By focusing on user-centered design principles, I developed a mobile-first application that streamlines complex real estate operations while building trust and credibility for Channel Partners.

The key challenge was designing for a diverse user base—from individual brokers to organizational teams—while maintaining simplicity and efficiency. The Digital Visiting Card feature, integrated CRM, and Commission Management system demonstrate how thoughtful UX design can professionalize an industry and create meaningful business impact. This project reinforced my understanding of how technology can transform traditional business models, particularly in sectors like real estate that rely heavily on personal relationships and trust. The experience highlighted the importance of balancing functionality with usability, ensuring that powerful business tools remain accessible to users with varying technical expertise.
