Interviews

In-depth interviews were conducted with ASHA workers and ANMs to understand their daily workflows, pain points with existing health management systems, and specific needs related to maternal and child health tracking.

Surveys

Surveys were distributed among a larger group of healthcare workers to gather quantitative data on their preferences, usage patterns, and technological capabilities.

Field Observations

Observations were carried out in the field to observe how healthcare workers interacted with patients, managed health records, and utilized existing tools and technologies.
